Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

HY3N ("we", "our", or "us") operates the HY3N Rider and HY3N Driver mobile applications. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Name, phone number, and email address when you register.
  • Location data: Real-time GPS location during trips to match you with drivers and provide navigation.
  • Trip data: Pickup and drop-off locations, trip duration, distance, and fare information.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system, and app usage data for service improvement.
  • Payment information: Transaction records (we do not store full payment card details).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To connect passengers with nearby drivers.
  • To process trip requests and calculate fares.
  • To ensure safety and monitor trip quality.
  • To send trip confirmations and service notifications.
  • To improve our platform and customer support.

3. Location Data

We collect precise location data while the app is in use to provide core ride-hailing functionality. Location data is used solely to match drivers and passengers, provide real-time tracking, and calculate accurate routes and fares. We do not sell location data to third parties.

4. Data Sharing

We share limited data only as necessary:

  • Drivers receive passenger pickup location and first name for trip coordination.
  • Passengers see driver name, vehicle details, and real-time location during a trip.
  • We do not sell personal data to advertisers or third parties.

5. Data Retention

Trip and account data is retained for up to 3 years for safety, legal compliance, and dispute resolution purposes. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS) and secure cloud storage to protect your personal information.

7. Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

8. Children's Privacy

HY3N is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or by email.
